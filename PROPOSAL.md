# Real Estate Dad — Product Proposal

*An operating system for real estate investing — not another property-search tool.*

---

## The Idea in One Line

Most tools show you listings. **Real Estate Dad finds the opportunity, explains why it exists, tells you exactly what to do next, and tracks it from discovery all the way to closing** — like having an experienced investing mentor and a research team built into one app.

---

## The Problem

Serious investors — especially in off-market deals (tax-delinquent, tax liens, tax deeds, probate, foreclosure, land) — lose most of their time to grunt work:

- Digging through county records one property at a time
- Rebuilding the same analysis spreadsheet for every deal
- Guessing which strategy fits
- Figuring out *who* to call and *what* to ask
- Losing track of deadlines, documents, and follow-ups

The data exists. What's missing is a system that does the finding, thinking, and organizing for you.

---

## The Solution

Real Estate Dad runs the entire investing workflow:

1. **You set a wealth goal.** The app builds a strategy and a "Buy Box" that fits it.
2. **The app finds the deals** — it searches public records and off-market sources for you. You don't pull lists.
3. **It scores every property** — overall, strategy fit, risk, capital fit, timeline, confidence.
4. **It explains each one** — why it's flagged, strengths, weaknesses, risks, upside, best strategy, exit options.
5. **It builds your action plan** — due-diligence checklist, who to contact, questions to ask, documents to request.
6. **It tracks the whole deal** — every call, letter, offer, and document, from discovery to portfolio.

The AI coaches like a mentor: *"Here's why this opportunity exists. Here's what concerns me. Here's what I'd verify before spending a dollar. Here's what I'd do next."*

**It never hypes, never pressures, and never fabricates numbers** — every figure shows where it came from.

## Core Features

- **Wealth Planner** — turn income/cash/credit/goals into a strategy and acquisition roadmap
- **Property Explorer** — search by address, ZIP, county, city; filter by distress signal
- **Opportunity Score** — six-factor scoring on every property
- **Deal Analysis** — the full "why," strengths, risks, upside, strategy, exits
- **Action Plan** — auto-built checklists for research, due diligence, outreach, negotiation
- **Contact Map** — who's involved in this deal type, why, and what to ask them
- **Land Intelligence** — parcels, zoning, subdivision, development, flood/utilities
- **Calculators** — Rental, BRRRR, Flip, Wholesale, Cash Flow, ROI, Cap Rate, NOI, Max Offer, Rehab
- **AI Coach** — mentor-style guidance on every page
- **Task Center & Alerts** — deadlines, follow-ups, new-match notifications
- **Opportunity Timeline** — a living history of every property; ask *"what happened with the Bowie duplex?"* months later and get the full story

## Smart Automation (Phase 2)

- **Autonomous nightly agent** — while you sleep, it pulls new county records, scores them against your Buy Box, and hands you the top deals each morning with explanations.
- **Learns your taste** — watches which deals you pursue vs. skip and sharpens its recommendations over time.
- **Document reader** — upload a deed, tax bill, or probate filing; it extracts the key facts into the deal record.

## What It Costs to Run

You already own the core stack (hosting, database, AI). **The only running cost is property data**, and it's pay-for-what-you-use:

- **$0 upfront**
- **~$20–100/month** depending on how actively you're working deals
- Automated county-record workers do the free sourcing; you only pay the data API for things like owner contact info — and only when you approve it

Compare to PropStream ($99/mo) or DealMachine ($99+/mo) — flat fees, no coaching, and you do the searching.
