Kept Honest

The app automates research and admin. It does not replace attorneys, appraisers, inspectors, or closing agents where their licensed role is legally required, and a real estate agent is still worth hiring on some deals, such as listing after a flip or buying on the MLS. The point is that most direct tax, probate, and land deals go owner to closing without an agent. The app tells you which is which, so you only pay a commission when a deal truly calls for one.

Included bonus

SignFlow: Run Every Job From Lead to Paid.

On top of the investing app, you get SignFlow at no extra cost. It is a complete contractor operations platform, a mobile app that manages the full lifecycle of a job: lead, booking, estimate, client signature, crew execution, sign-offs, client acceptance, payment, and warranty. Every major step writes to an append-only audit trail, so nothing is lost and nothing is disputed.

1

Leads to Booked Jobs

A pre-booking pipeline for website, phone, referral, Google, and Facebook leads, plus Google Calendar import that turns an appointment into a booked job in one click.

2

Estimates and Client Portal

Build an estimate with line items, tax, deposit, and site photos, then send it. The client reviews it through a secure link with no login, then approves, requests changes, or signs on the screen. Signing turns the estimate into an active project on its own.

3

Crew Execution

Invite estimators and crew, gated behind signed agreement packs. Crews clock in and out, add progress photos and notes, and complete midpoint and final sign-offs with a checklist and signature.

4

Quality and Acceptance

A project manager inspection stands between the crew's final sign-off and the client. The client does a final walkthrough and signs acceptance, which unlocks the final invoice.

5

Payments and Change Orders

Deposit on signing, progress payments, and a final payment gated behind client approval. Change orders capture a client signature and create the matching payment automatically.

6

Warranty and Audit Trail

Final payment starts a one-year warranty with tracked claims. Job Replay steps back through every photo, document, signature, and payment from the exact moment it happened.

SignFlow also handles canvas e-signatures, in-app plus email and SMS notifications, a per-project health score, project chat, and installs on a phone like an app. It is a platform normally sold on a monthly subscription. Here it is bundled into the one-time build, yours to run at no monthly fee.
